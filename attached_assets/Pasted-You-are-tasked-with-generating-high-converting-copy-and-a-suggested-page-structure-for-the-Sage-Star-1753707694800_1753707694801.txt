You are tasked with generating high‑converting copy and a suggested page structure for the Sage‑Startups pre‑launch landing page. This page will collect email addresses for a wait‑list. Your goal is to:

Create compelling, benefit‑driven copy

Start with a hero headline that tells visitors they can “Turn your idea into a launch‑ready business in minutes.”

Write a short sub‑headline explaining that Sage‑Startups uses AI to generate business plans, branding and logos, marketing content and automated market research.

Add a clear call‑to‑action (CTA) like “Join the waitlist” with supporting text such as “No credit card required.”

Recommend page sections and content

Hero section: headline, sub‑headline, bullet list of 3–4 benefits, and an email sign‑up form.

Feature section: create cards or short paragraphs for each capability:

AI‑generated business plans.

Brand & logo creation.

Marketing & content generation.

Market research and competitor analysis.

Idea validation & roadmap planning.

How it works: provide a simple 3‑step process (input idea → AI generates plans/branding/insights → review and refine).

Wait‑list benefits: outline perks for early adopters (early access, founder discounts, ability to influence development, community access).

Roadmap or “coming soon” section: mention upcoming features like pitch‑deck creation, website builder integration, SEO keyword suggestions and 24/7 support.

Testimonials/social proof: suggest adding quotes from beta testers or badges like “Featured on WhatTheAI.”

FAQ: answer common questions about what the platform does, when it will launch, data privacy and pricing.

Advise on pricing presentation

Mention that comparable AI planning tools cost $12–$30 per month and up to $40 for premium tiers.

Propose either (a) showing a price range and promoting an early‑bird lifetime plan (e.g., a one‑time fee in the ~£30 range) or (b) focusing on benefits and offering wait‑list members a significant discount without revealing the exact price.

Suggest including a free trial or limited free tier.

Add closing CTA and footer suggestions

End with another sign‑up form and links to social accounts or a blog.

Encourage visitors to engage by teasing the product roadmap or inviting them to join a founder community.

Use clear, persuasive language and keep the tone professional yet approachable. Include all major points above in your output.
